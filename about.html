01 : About me

Boston University

Carlton Knox graduated from Boston University in 2023 with a B.S. in Comptuer Engineering. While at BU, Carlton focused on VLSI, Computer Architecture, Operating Systems, and HPC, working as part of the Performance and Energy Aware Computing Lab (PEACLab), and the Computer Architecture and Automated Design Lab (CAADLab). He also led the BU High Performance Computing Club, and was a treasurer for the Society of Hispanic Professional Engineers.

AMD

Currently, Carlton is working at AMD as of the Server Performance Group, where he works on the EPYC line of server class CPUs. He originally interned with AMD in the summer of 2022, and returned to the team as a full time employee in August of 2023.

Carlton also loves cooking new recipes, making coffee/tea, taking pictures, playing videogames, and listening to jazz.
